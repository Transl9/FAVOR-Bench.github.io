            <h2>Abstract</h2>

            <div class="row">

                <div class="col-lg-8">

                    <p>

                        FAVOR-Bench is a comprehensive benchmark designed to evaluate large language models 

                        across multiple dimensions including factuality, reasoning, understanding, and robustness.

                        Our benchmark provides a standardized way to assess model performance and helps

                        identify strengths and weaknesses in current state-of-the-art language models.

                    </p>

            <h2>Methodology</h2>

            <div class="row">

                <div class="col-md-6">

                    <p>

                        FAVOR-Bench evaluates models across four key dimensions:

                    </p>

                    <ul>

                        <li><strong>Factuality</strong>: Assessing if model outputs contain accurate information</li>

                        <li><strong>Adaptability</strong>: Measuring model performance across diverse domains</li>

                        <li><strong>Versatility</strong>: Evaluating models on different types of reasoning tasks</li>

                        <li><strong>Robustness</strong>: Testing model stability against adversarial inputs</li>

                    </ul>

                </div>
