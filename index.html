Introduction

FAVOR-Bench is a fine-grained audio-visual object relational understanding benchmark designed to evaluate multimodal large language models (MLLMs) on their ability to comprehend complex relationships between objects in audio-visual content. The benchmark focuses on challenging tasks that require integrating information from both visual and audio modalities to accurately understand object interactions, attributes, and relationships.

Motivation

Current multimodal models excel at basic object recognition but struggle with complex relational understanding across audio and visual modalities. FAVOR-Bench addresses this gap by providing a comprehensive evaluation framework that tests models on their ability to understand fine-grained object relations, interactions, and attributes in multimodal contexts. This benchmark is designed to push the boundaries of multimodal AI systems toward more human-like understanding of audiovisual content.
